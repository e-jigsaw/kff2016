Innocent Prayer

2015 / Japan / 85min

Director, Screenplay: Toru Kamei

Original: Hirayama Yumeaki

Starring: Fukuda Miki, BB Goro, Ai Shimomura

"Kill them all, I hope he kills everyone". Fumi is a miserable girl who is bullied at school, and is abused at home. In this world where only despair exist, the 10-year-old girl asked for help was a serial killer. Based on the novel by Yumeaki Hirayama. It is made into complete imaging in relentless contents.

[Talk event] Yumeaki Hirayama × Toru Kamei
